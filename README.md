### 👋 About Me

I'm **Vishnu Vardhan Rao Badhrapu**, a Data & Business Intelligence professional with **4 years** of experience turning messy, scattered data into dashboards, pipelines, and decisions that actually move the needle. I move comfortably across Data Analytics, BI, Operations, Product, and ERP (SAP/Oracle) — wherever the data lives, I go find it, clean it, and make it talk.

Outside the day job, I create content to make data and tech more accessible — videos on YouTube, deep-dive write-ups on Medium, and bite-sized tips on Instagram.

- 📊 I think in tables, joins, and dashboards before I think in words
- 🔁 I've automated more repetitive Excel tasks than I can count — and loved every minute of it
- 🧩 ERP systems (SAP/Oracle) don't scare me; I actually enjoy untangling them
- ☕ Powered by SQL queries, dashboards, and an unreasonable amount of coffee
